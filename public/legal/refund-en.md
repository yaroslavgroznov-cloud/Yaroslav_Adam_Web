## 3. How to request a refund

Submit a request in **free form** to `support@groznov.net` indicating:

- **`order_id`** or **account email** used for payment;
- **scenario** from the table above (the reason for the request);
- **desired outcome** — refund or re-provision of service.

**Review period:** up to **5 (five) business days** from the date the request is received.

**Refund method:** to the same payment instrument used for payment (card via LiqPay/Lemon Squeezy/Fondy; for crypto — subject to the corresponding §2 sub-clause).

## 4. Subscription cancellation

The User may cancel a subscription at any time via:

- **Personal account** at `https://adam.groznov.uk/account`;
- **Email to `support@groznov.net`** with the subject "Cancel subscription" and the account email.

Cancellation stops automatic renewal. Access is retained until the end of the paid period. If the User wishes a refund for the unused period — a request is additionally submitted per §3.

## 6. Special cases

### 6.1. Erroneous payment

If the User mistakenly paid for a duplicate Session or an unwanted Subscription — a full refund is possible provided:
- the request is submitted within **24 hours** of payment;
- the service has not been used (no interaction with Adam has occurred).

### 6.2. Technical failure after payment

If the User made payment but access to the Room/Subscription did not activate due to technical reasons on the Platform's side — a refund is processed **automatically** after confirmation of the incident, or the service is re-provided at the User's choice.

### 6.3. Service materially not matching the description

If the User believes that a thematic Room materially does not match its description on `https://adam.groznov.uk/pricing` — they are entitled to a refund for a single Session within 14 days of payment, even if interaction with AI occurred. The request is reviewed individually.
